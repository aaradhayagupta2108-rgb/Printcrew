🔒 Privacy Policy

What we collect: When you upload a file, page thumbnails (low-resolution previews) are generated and stored for order management purposes.

How long: Thumbnails are retained for 30 days from order date, then permanently deleted.

Who can see it: Only PrintCrew admin can view order thumbnails. Your files are never shared with third parties.

Your rights: You can request deletion of your order data by contacting PrintCrew admin with your Order ID.

Data location: Order data is stored on PrintCrew's secure servers.

Compliance: PrintCrew operates in accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

FAQs

What file formats can I print?›
We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, JPG, PNG, and TXT. For best results, upload PDFs.
How does the vending machine work?›
Upload your file, choose print settings, complete payment — the nearest PrintCrew machine prints automatically. Walk up and collect.
Are my documents private?›
Your files are processed for printing and page thumbnails are stored for 30 days for admin verification, then permanently deleted. Files are never shared with third parties.
What if the machine doesn't print after payment?›
Contact PrintCrew support with your Order ID. We'll verify payment and either reprocess or issue a full refund.
How long does printing take?›
The machine starts within seconds of payment confirmation. Most jobs complete in under 2 minutes.
How do I get a refund?›
Contact PrintCrew admin with your Order ID. Refunds are processed within 24–48 hours for verified issues.
What is the max number of files?›
Up to 10 files per order, each up to 20 MB.
